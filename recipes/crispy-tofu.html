Crispy tofu

Description

Crispy tofu inspired by Korean cuisine.

Ingredients

Crispy tofu

  • 1 block firm or extra-firm tofu
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil or other neutral oil
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch or potato starch
  • pinch salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Drain the block of extra-firm tofu and use your favorite method to press out the excess moisture for at least 15 minutes. I like to use my tofu press.
  2. Cut the tofu into cubes or tear it into bite-sized pieces and add them to a bowl.
  3. Drizzle vegetable oil over the tofu pieces. Sprinkle on salt and black pepper (to preference). Toss to evenly coat.
  4. The tofu can be a bit delicate, so a flexible rubber spatula can be helpful to gently toss without the tofu crumbling too much. Alternatively, if you have a container with a lid, you can gently shake to coat.
  5. Sprinkle the cornstarch (or potato starch) over the seasoned tofu. Again, gently toss or shake to coat.
